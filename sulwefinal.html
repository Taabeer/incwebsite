Sulwe lesson



Task 1: Listen to the read along of the book: Sulwe by Lupita Nyong'o

Task 2: Test your comprehension by doing this fun quiz!

Task 3: Empowering word search adjectives

After finding all the words in the word search, use three words from the word search to describe yourself and three words from the word search to describe the person next to you

Task 4: Online research

Search up your name meaning online, try to find the origins and history of your name and any other fun facts

After, share with the class!
